About INSIGHT

Intelligent Systems are playing an increasingly important role in clinical practice, supporting physicians and healthcare professionals in disease diagnosis, therapy optimization, and health monitoring. Among these advancements, a promising research direction is imaging-based diagnosis, where intelligent systems enable accurate, robust, and timely interpretation of medical images.

This Special Session, \textit{INSIGHT}, aims to bring together recent advances in AI-driven image analysis for healthcare, focusing on methods and systems that support imaging-based clinical decision making across key application domains (\textit{e.g.}, radiology, digital and computational pathology, computational cytology, and neuroimaging). Contributions addressing both methodological advances and clinically oriented applications are encouraged.

  • Federated / privacy-preserving learning

Call for Papers

We invite high-quality submissions on intelligent systems and machine learning methods for imaging-based diagnosis in healthcare. Topics include (but are not limited to):

AI for medical imaging diagnosis

  • Computer-aided diagnosis and triage
  • Detection/segmentation/classification pipelines
  • Self-supervised and weakly supervised learning
  • Foundation models and transfer learning

Trustworthy & clinically usable AI

  • Explainability, interpretability, causality-inspired methods
  • Uncertainty quantification & reliability
  • Bias, fairness, and safety in clinical settings
  • Human-AI collaboration and workflow integration

Multimodal & data-centric approaches

  • Imaging + EHR + omics integration
  • Longitudinal modeling and outcome prediction
  • Data harmonization and domain adaptation
  • Synthetic data & generative models

Privacy-preserving learning

  • Federated learning for imaging
  • Secure / distributed training pipelines
  • Confidential computing, differential privacy
  • Regulatory and reproducibility aspects
